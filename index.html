ELKO lectors and topic description
ELKO 30. Before. Now. After.
Discover how distribution has evolved, where we stand today, and our plans for the following years. Let's talk about products, services, and team evolution management. What should we expect in the future?
Andrejs Gavrilovs
Sales Office Director Baltics and Poland, ELKO GROUP
Low energy? Three simple (but not easy) things to help you to energize
What main factors influence our energy level and how we feel in our daily life. How to master your daily routines and behaviours to feel more energised instead of being tired, stay calm instead of stressing out and feeling alive instead of pushing through days.
Migle Rudmine
Director of ELKO Lithuania / MBSR, MBCT teacher / Permanent Life student & practitioner
Relationship with AI
The lecture will cover the most often used AI technologies in the IT industry, highlight how it changed the industry in 2023 and what to expect in the future.
Artis Krilovs
CEO of the creative digital agency WRONG Digital
Relationship With Trust
Creating a psychologically safe environment in the conversation, to be united in respect and trust. Body language, impact of words, behavior statuses. Practical approach.
Aija Iesalniece
Communication, speech, cooperation, and leadership consultant  / Management psychology / Improvisation model trainer
